**MosTune Privacy Policy**
**Effective Date: February 2026**

Thank you for choosing MosTune. This Privacy Policy outlines how data is handled when you use the MosTune Discord bot. By adding or interacting with MosTune, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

---

### 1. Information We Collect

MosTune is built to deliver reliable and high-quality music features while keeping data collection minimal. The bot may process and store limited technical data such as:

* **Guild (Server) IDs** – to apply server-specific settings and configurations
* **User IDs** – for command execution, premium features (e.g., no-prefix access), and misuse prevention
* **Voice Channel IDs** – required to connect and manage music playback sessions
* **Configuration Data** – including volume levels, filters, autoplay, and feature preferences
* **Usage Metadata** – basic command activity used for performance optimization and abuse detection

MosTune does **not** collect or retain personal information such as private messages, passwords, or sensitive user content.

---

### 2. How Data Is Used

The data collected is strictly used to operate and improve MosTune, including:

* Delivering music playback and related features
* Enabling premium functionalities
* Monitoring and preventing spam, abuse, or malicious use
* Enhancing performance, stability, and user experience
* Diagnosing errors and providing support

MosTune does not sell, trade, or share collected data with external parties.

---

### 3. Command Handling & Messages

* Commands are processed instantly to perform requested actions
* Message content is not stored permanently
* Limited technical logs (such as command type or IDs) may be kept briefly for debugging and safety purposes

---

### 4. Data Protection

We apply reasonable safeguards to protect stored data from unauthorized access or misuse.

* Data retention is limited to what is necessary for functionality
* Outdated or unused data is periodically cleared

---

### 5. Access & Control

* Server administrators control how MosTune operates within their server
* Developers may access restricted data only when necessary for maintenance, troubleshooting, or improvements

This Privacy Policy may be revised from time to time. Continued use of MosTune after any updates indicates acceptance of the revised terms.
